USL Web Extension

The USL Web Extension is a convenient tool that allows you to quickly and easily add the URL of the current page you are visiting to The Ultimate Survival Library's collection of survival and prepping resources. With just a click, you can submit valuable websites to be included in our extensive library, helping to build a comprehensive resource for all survival enthusiasts.

This extension has been reviewed and approved by the Google Chrome Store, ensuring it meets all necessary guidelines and security standards.

Our Privacy Policy explains our use of the URL you submit.
