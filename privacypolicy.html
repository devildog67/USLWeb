Privacy Policy for the USL Web URL Sender Chrome Extension

What Information Do You Collect?

The USL Web URL Sender Chrome extension collects only one type of information: the URL of the website that the user wishes to submit to uslweb.com. This information is collected when the user clicks the extension icon and confirms their intent to submit the URL. The extension does not collect any other data, such as personal information, browsing history, or any form of user-sensitive data. No data is automatically collected; all data collection is initiated by the user.

How Do You Use the Information?

The collected URL is transmitted to the uslweb.com API for review and consideration for inclusion in the uslweb.com collection of survival and prepping links. This is the sole purpose of collecting the URL. The information is not used to recognize users, track usage, or for any promotional purposes. The URL is stored securely on our servers and is reviewed by our team to ensure it aligns with the content and quality standards of uslweb.com.

What Information Do You Share?

The USL Web URL Sender Chrome extension does not share any collected information with third parties. The URL submitted by the user is solely used within the uslweb.com infrastructure for review and potential inclusion in our collection. We do not share, sell, or disclose this information to any external entities. The data is used exclusively for enhancing the uslweb.com repository and improving the resources available to our users.

By using the USL Web URL Sender extension, you agree to the collection and use of your submitted URLs as described in this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure and transparent process for submitting URLs. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@uslweb.com.
